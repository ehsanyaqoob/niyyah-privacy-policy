# Niyyah Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

## What We Collect
- Email address (if you sign in)
- Prayer logs you create
- Your approximate location (for prayer time calculation only)

## What We Don't Do
- We never sell your data
- We never share your data with third parties
- Location is used only for prayer time calculation and never stored on our servers

## Data Storage
- Prayer logs are stored locally on your device
- If you sign in, logs are backed up to Supabase (supabase.com)
- You can delete your account and all data at any time from Settings
